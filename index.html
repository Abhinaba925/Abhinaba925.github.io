About Me

I'm a researcher driven by a first-principles approach, leveraging deep mathematical structures to build robust, intelligent, data-driven solutions across machine learning, quantum computing, and quantitative finance.

From modeling fundamental laws of nature at IISER Kolkata, to engineering real-world optical communication systems at IIT Delhi, to diving deep into modern AI at IIT Bombay's C-MInDS — my journey has always been about building a multidisciplinary foundation to tackle complex problems at their core.

I believe the most powerful solutions emerge when you understand the physics beneath the math, and the math beneath the code.
