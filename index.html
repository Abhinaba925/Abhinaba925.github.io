About Me

I'm a researcher at the intersection of quantum computing, machine learning, and quantitative finance — building systems where physics meets profit and theory meets production.

My work spans from solving Black-Scholes PDEs with neural networks to implementing hybrid quantum-classical optimization algorithms. I believe the next frontier in finance will be written in qubits.

When I'm not collapsing wavefunctions or training models, you'll find me exploring new alpha signals, reading arxiv papers, or breaking things to understand how they work.
